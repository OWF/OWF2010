11 March 2010 - OPEN WORLD FORUM 2010: Open Is The Future 

" Year on year, the Free / Open Source model is affirming itself as the invisible driver behind the digital revolution, whether at the level of emerging technologies (Cloud computing, the Internet of Things...) or content. Over and above our exploration of free / open software and open innovation – the foundation stones of the OWF – we believe it is essential to extend our thinking in 2010, to explore the possible impact of the open model on the economic and social challenges we face. In line with our aim to be the 'Davos' of the world of open technologies, the Open World Forum is positioning itself more than ever as a space where ideas and initiatives can be fully explored. We call on everyone involved in the IT world to join us in this unique global initiative. "
